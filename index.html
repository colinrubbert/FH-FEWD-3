About
Designer. Developer. Freelancer.

I may be grown up but I will forever be young at heart and have the curiousity of child. This curiosity and enthusiasm has given me the passion to explore anything that I find interesting. Finding new challenges and activities to expand my knowledge and skillset will also be who I am.

Having many passions, interests, and experiences have given me a unique insight and view of the world. I share these experiences through my writing and photography. Stick around for some personal insights, stories, and visuals of my life.
